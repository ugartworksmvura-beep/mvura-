Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about mvura, accounts, purchases, and game publishing.

Getting Started

What is mvura?

mvura is a game distribution platform based in Malawi that connects players with developers. We offer a wide range of games for mobile, PC, console, and web. Our mission is to provide transparent, fair, and sustainable digital distribution.

Do I need an account to download games?

No, you can browse and download free games without an account. However, creating a free account allows you to purchase games, track your downloads, leave reviews, and if you're a developer, publish your own games.

Is mvura free to use?

Yes, creating an account and browsing the store is completely free. Many games are also free to download. We only charge for games marked as "Paid" or "Premium", and developers pay a 15% platform fee on their sales.

Accounts & Profile

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign In" button in the top menu, then select "Create Account". You'll need to provide your first name, last name, phone number (Malawi format: 099xxxxxxx or +2659xxxxxxxx), and email address. Choose a password and select your role (Player or Developer). You must agree to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. After clicking "Sign up", you'll receive a verification code via SMS (simulated in demo) to confirm your phone number. Once verified, you'll be logged in automatically.

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

On the login modal, click "Forgot password?". Enter your phone number and the email address you used to register. You'll receive a 6‑digit reset code (check the browser console – this is a demo feature). Enter the code and your new password to reset it.

How do I change my profile information?

Once logged in, open the menu and click on "Account". There you can edit your first name, last name, email, bio (for developers), website, and upload a profile picture. Don't forget to click "Save Changes".

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account from the Account page. Scroll to the bottom and click "Delete Account". Please note that this action is permanent and will remove your profile, reviews, and any unpublished games. If you're a developer with published games, you must contact support to handle existing content.

Buying & Downloading Games

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept Airtel Money and TNM Mpamba (Malawi's leading mobile money services). All prices are displayed in Malawian Kwacha (MWK). We plan to add additional payment options in the future.

How does the payment process work?

When you click "Buy Now" on a game, a payment modal appears. Select your payment method (Airtel Money or TNM Mpamba), enter your phone number (e.g., 0991234567), and confirm. You will receive a USSD prompt on your phone to authorise the payment. After successful payment, your download will start automatically.

Can I get a refund?

Due to the digital nature of games, purchases are generally non‑refundable. However, if a game is defective or not as described, you may request a refund within 14 days by contacting support@mvura.games. Each request will be evaluated individually. Statutory rights under the Malawi Consumer Protection Act apply.

Where are my downloaded games stored?

Games are downloaded directly to your device. On mobile, they will typically be in your "Downloads" folder. On PC, you can choose the download location. You can also access your purchased games anytime from your Library.

What if my download fails?

If a download fails, you can retry from your Library. If the problem persists, check your internet connection or contact support with the game name and your account details.

Developers & Publishing

How do I become a developer?

If you signed up as a player, you can upgrade to a developer role from the Publish page. Click "Upgrade to Developer" – this will instantly change your role. If you're creating a new account, select "Developer" as your role during signup.

How much does it cost to publish a game?

Publishing a game on mvura is completely free. There are no upfront fees. We only take a 15% platform fee on revenue generated from game sales. You keep 85% of every sale.

How do I upload my game?

Once you're a developer, go to the Publish page and click "Start Upload". Fill in the game details (title, description, genre, price, etc.), provide cover art and screenshots, and upload your game file (APK for mobile, ZIP for PC/console). You also need to provide a Backblaze B2 file key – this is where your game file is stored. After submission, our team will review your game (usually within 1‑3 business days).

What are the revenue payouts?

Payouts are processed monthly. You'll receive 85% of the net revenue from your game sales. The minimum payout threshold is MWK 5,000. Payments are sent via Airtel Money or TNM Mpamba (the same method you use for purchases). You can track your earnings in the Developer Dashboard.

Can I update my game after publishing?

Yes. In your Developer Dashboard, you'll find options to upload new versions of your games. Each version will go through a review process before being published to ensure quality and security.

Technical Support

The website is slow or not loading properly.

First, try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the issue persists, check your internet connection. You can also try a different browser. If the problem continues, contact support with details of the issue.

I'm having trouble with a game I downloaded.

Check the game's system requirements to ensure your device is compatible. If the game crashes or has bugs, you can leave a review and contact the developer directly through their profile. For issues with the download file, contact support@mvura.games.

How do I report a bug on the mvura platform?

Please email support@mvura.games with a description of the bug, steps to reproduce it, and your browser/device information. Screenshots are very helpful.

Privacy & Legal

How do you protect my personal information?

We take data protection seriously. All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS. We store personal information securely and never share it with third parties without your consent, except as required by law. For full details, please read our Privacy Policy.

What are cookies and how do you use them?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. We also use analytics cookies (via Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our site, which helps us improve. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings. See our Cookie Policy for more.

How do I report copyright infringement?

If you believe a game on mvura infringes your copyright, please send a takedown notice to copyright@mvura.games with the required information (see our DMCA page for details). We will respond promptly.
